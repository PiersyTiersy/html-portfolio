Favorite Touhou 19 Music

Top 5 Songs of UDOALG

The Oni Go to the Perpetual Mountain

Love the unique chord progression, and agressive, yet low Blow Tenor. The Piano is also doing a bunch of 9ths which sound great, and give the theme a rather unique touch.

The Path to Yomi Where None Turn Back

The beautiful harmony running through the entirety of this song is majestic, powerful, dark, and filled with beauty. not to mention the astounding piano work. This song is 9th heaven. Also love the Dulcimer

The Deviants' Unobstructed Light ~ Kingdom of Nothingness

The Mongolian Chants truly make this theme one of a kind. The emotional weight running through the entirety of this song is rather unique, compared to Pure Furies or Little Princess, thanks to its rather unusual "hidden", or "drowned by emotion" approach to power

A Beast's Intelligence

Powerful and emotional. The Main chorus feels so warm and familiar thanks to the main motif + VI - VII - i. The jazzy part with the violin and no piano, is refreshing and really unique.

Magical Beast Scramble

Incredible Piano, unique use of st. strings, peculiar rythms (9/8,7/8), and that AMAZING ending make this theme stand out a lot.
